\section{Runge's Theorem}
Let $K \subset \mathbb{C}$ be compact and let $f(z)$ be a function holomorphic on $\Omega \supset K$ open.
Then there exists a sequence  $ R_n$ of rational functions, poles not on  $K$ such that
 \[
     \sup_{K} |R_n(z) - f(z)| \xrightarrow{n \to \infty} 0
\]
